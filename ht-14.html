Your spine is an essential part of your body because it enables you to breathe well, allows proper movement, and protects your spinal cord. Family physiotherapists can use spinal manipulation to effectively address any problem whenever you feel pain in your spine or other parts of your body.

Why is spinal manipulation beneficial for your condition?

The structure of the spine looks complicated. It comprises discs, ligaments, vertebrae, tendons, nerves, muscles, and joints. To ensure the spine functions well, it must attain a balanced state in order to function well and improve your health condition. When a patient begins to live with pain, the spine can be affected. And that’s why we introduce spinal manipulation therapy to help relieve the pain and ensure the spine is at its optimal state to have balanced spinal mobility and spinal stability. With this said if you are in Edmonton AB feel free to contact us anytime you want, our therapists are specialized in multiple areas of treating back pain, give us a try.

Our spinal manipulation services are performed by specialized physiotherapists. If you feel any pain or your spine that makes you feel uncomfortable, we request you, contact us immediately. What else should I know about spinal manipulation?

Globally, spinal manipulation has been a very effective therapeutic care in relieving both acute and chronic pain. According to a report from the International Journal of Sports Physiotherapy, spinal manipulation is a very effective treatment method. It further concluded that the in-depth research of this systematic review supports the adoption of spinal manipulation as a form of therapeutic care…. in clinical practice. [It] appears to be a safe intervention that improves clinical outcomes for patients with low back pain.”
