Conditions that can be treated using deep tissue massage

Deep tissue massage is a non-invasive treatment that is effective for some specific conditions ranging from acute to chronic state. Here at Family Physiotherapy, some of the conditions that can require deep tissue massage include;

  • Sports injuries
  • Soft tissue damage
  • Lower back strains
  • Muscle tension from sustained posture
  • Muscle sprains and strains

What is the aim of deep tissue massage therapy?

The focus of this treatment plan is to help stimulate the muscles around deep tissues. Using both hands on local or general muscle spasms increases the lactic cycle and muscle metabolism to ease the pain within the affected part of the body. We use deep, rapid, short-duration percussion when performing this therapy to eradicate pain effectively.
